# AI-Powered SEO Content Production Research

This repository contains research collected for the topic **AI-powered SEO content production**.

The purpose of this research is to understand how modern SEO content is changing in the age of AI search, answer engines, generative engines, and LLM-based discovery. Instead of studying generic SEO advice, I focused on experts and channels that are actively discussing how content should now be researched, structured, optimized, updated, and cited across platforms like Google, ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, Claude, and Google AI Overviews.

## What I Collected

I collected research from **10 high-signal sources** across LinkedIn and YouTube.

The collection includes:

- A curated list of experts and channels
- LinkedIn posts organized by creator
- YouTube video notes and transcript-based research
- Profile-level notes explaining why each source was selected
- Post and video-level notes explaining the topic, key idea, and relevance to AI-powered SEO content production

## Selection Logic

The experts were selected based on relevance to the research topic, not popularity alone.

I chose sources that met one or more of the following criteria:

1. They actively discuss **AI SEO, AEO, GEO, LLM visibility, or AI search behavior**.
2. They share practical workflows involving tools like **Claude, ChatGPT, Google Search Console, Perplexity, Gemini, or Google AI Overviews**.
3. They focus on how content should be structured for **retrieval, citations, answer extraction, and authority**.
4. They connect content production with real SEO outcomes such as **ranking, visibility, traffic, conversions, and revenue**.
5. They provide recent, specific, and implementation-focused insights instead of generic SEO tips.

This matters because AI-powered SEO content production is not just about writing content with AI. It involves understanding how AI systems discover content, interpret structure, evaluate authority, extract answers, and decide which sources to cite.
