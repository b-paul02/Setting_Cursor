- **Topic:** Short-form content and AI search
- **Key idea:** Irina argues that long-form content is losing effectiveness in AI search. Based on AirOps research, she says pages over 5,000 words get cited less often by ChatGPT than pages under 500 words, with the sweet spot around 500 to 2,000 words. Her main point is that short, clear, compressed content works better for both humans and LLMs.
- **Why it matters for AI-powered SEO content production:** This matters because AI-powered SEO content production should focus on clarity, compression, and usefulness instead of simply producing long articles. It suggests that content teams should create concise, direct, high-value pages that AI tools can easily understand, extract, and cite.
