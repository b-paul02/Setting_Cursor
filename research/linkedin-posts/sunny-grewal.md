## Post 3
- **Date:** 19th April 2026
- **Post link:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/sunny-grewal_seo-vs-aeo-vs-geo-updated-methods-activity-7450037756078125056-JKaQ?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAADbafy8BLR7CG_of6vm1lvATyLHBuQYWtMg
- **Topic:** SEO vs AEO vs GEO
- **Key idea:** Sunny explains the difference between SEO, AEO, and GEO in simple terms. SEO is about typing words and getting links, AEO is about asking a question and getting one reply, and GEO is about AI tools like ChatGPT answering the user. His advice is to use clear words for SEO, give quick answers for AEO, and aim to become the answer for GEO.
- **Why it matters for AI-powered SEO content production:** This post is relevant because it explains how content needs to be adapted for different search environments. AI-powered SEO content should be clear enough for traditional search, direct enough for answer engines, and authoritative enough to be selected by generative AI tools. This supports better structure, simpler language, answer-first writing, and AI-ready content formatting.
